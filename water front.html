Water Front (working title)

(in progress)

 

Right click to copy a texture, left click to paste it

First-person raycaster.

3D video game as Potemkin village: a trompe-l'oeil decoy construction exploiting the fraught relationships between photography and architecture, surface and structure, the linear eye of the tourist and the hidden dimensions of the city.

Players adopt the role of an officer-cum-urbanist who finds himself re-enacting the Potemkin myth, keeping one step ahead of an NPC inspector as she promenades downriver on an official inspection tour, embellishing facades along her route or even stealing her attention with some well-timed pyrotechnics.

I wrote about the project's influences and intentions in my doozy of an undergrad thesis.
