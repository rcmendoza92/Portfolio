Rabbi

(2016)

 

 

Footage from Genericon 2016 soundtracked to Bob Dylan's 1991 Grammy Lifetime Achievement Award acceptance speech, which paraphrased German Orthodox Rabbi Shimshon Raphael Hirsch.

I thought it would be fun to play with notions of piety, decadence, and the capacity for self-reinvention in the context of geek and fan culture.
